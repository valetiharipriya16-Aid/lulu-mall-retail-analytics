BUSINESS BACKGROUND
--------------------
Lulu Mall is one of India's fastest-growing retail chains, operating large-format
hypermarkets and shopping destinations across major cities including Bangalore,
Hyderabad, Chennai, Kochi, Mumbai, Delhi, Lucknow, and Coimbatore.

With rapid expansion across regions, the management team at Lulu Mall requires a
consolidated view of business performance to make data-driven decisions. The
leadership team has engaged a data analytics team (you) to analyze 12 months of
transactional sales data and deliver actionable insights through an Excel Dashboard.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
BUSINESS PROBLEM
-----------------
Lulu Mall management currently lacks a centralized analytics view and wants to
understand the following key areas:

  1. REVENUE PERFORMANCE
     - What is the total revenue generated across all stores?
     - Which months recorded the highest and lowest revenue?
     - Is overall revenue trending upward or downward over time?

  2. STORE PERFORMANCE
     - Which stores are the top performers by revenue?
     - Which stores are underperforming and may need strategic attention?
     - How does store performance vary across cities?

  3. CATEGORY PERFORMANCE
     - Which product categories generate the most revenue?
     - Which categories have the highest number of transactions?
     - Are there any categories that are consistently underperforming?

  4. CUSTOMER BEHAVIOR
     - What is the gender split of customers across stores?
     - What are the most preferred payment modes?
     - What is the average order value per customer segment?

  5. REGIONAL TRENDS
     - How does revenue compare across different states and regions?
     - Which regions (North, South, East, West) contribute most to total revenue?
     - Are there regional preferences in product categories?

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STUDENT OBJECTIVE
------------------
As an intern at Lulu Mall's Analytics Division, your task is to:

  - Explore and understand the sales dataset
  - Clean and validate the data
  - Apply Excel formulas to derive key business metrics
  - Use VLOOKUP / INDEX-MATCH for data enrichment
  - Build Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts to summarize data
  - Create a professional interactive Dashboard with KPI Cards and Slicers

The final deliverable must resemble a real business dashboard that a manager
can use to make quick, informed decisions.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
EXPECTED DELIVERABLES
----------------------
Students must submit the following inside a single Excel Workbook (.xlsx):

  Sheet 1:  Raw_Data          — Original dataset (do not modify)
  Sheet 2:  Working_Data      — Cleaned/enriched data with added formula columns
  Sheet 3:  Formula_Analysis  — Answers to formula-based business questions
  Sheet 4:  Lookup_Analysis   — VLOOKUP and INDEX+MATCH based lookups
  Sheet 5:  Pivot_Analysis    — Pivot Tables (at least 5)
  Sheet 6:  Dashboard         — Final interactive dashboard with KPIs and Charts
